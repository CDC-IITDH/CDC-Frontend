Frequently Asked Question


When does the internship and placement programmes start?

Placement drive and internship drive is starting from September 1, 2021.

Any fee associated with the process?

No, the companies are not required to pay any fees.

Brief about the courses offered at IITDh ?

IIT Dharwad has B.Tech. , MS and PhD programs. B.Tech has 3 departments Computer Science Engineering ,Electrical Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering each having a batch strength of 45, 40, and 35 respectively. MS and PhD from 3 departments Computer Science Engineering . Electrical Engineering. and Mechanical Engineering.

In which modes can the selection procedure be conducted? (offline/online)

Currently due to Covid situation, final and pre-final year students are not available on campus. So it would be good if the selections are done online.

The company has already filled the INF/JNF, now what is the next step?

On successful submission of INF/JNF we have received your details. We are in process of assigning a Public Relation officer for you, they will get in touch with you soon.
