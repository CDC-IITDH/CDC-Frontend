Frequently Asked Question


When does the internship and placement programmes start?

Placement drive and internship drive is starting from September 1, 2020.

Any fee associated with the process (which companies need to pay)?

No, the companies are not required to pay any fees.

How to reach IIT Dharwad?

IIT Dharwad is currently functioning from the transit campus within the Water and Land Management Institute (WALMI) which lies on NH 48 (PB Road) in Belur, 13 km from the heart of Dharwad city, towards Belgaum.
By Road
Since the campus is situated on NH 48 connecting Mumbai (546 km, 10.5-12 hours approx) and Bengaluru (436 km, 8-9 hours approx), you can catch a bus to reach the campus from these places. The buses from these cities drop you right in front of the campus if you ask for a request stop.
By Rail
Dharwad is connected to all the major cities of the country like Bangalore (428 km), Mumbai (559 km), Delhi (2127 km), Kolkata (1931 km), Chennai (832 km) and Hyderabad (683 km) by rail. 20 km away from Dharwad towards Bengaluru lies Hubballi, a major railway junction providing better connectivity.
By Air
There are three airports located near the campus â€“ Hubballi Airport (30 km), Belgaum Airport (75 km) and Dabolim Airport, Goa (166 km). While there are flights to/from Hubballi and Belgaum to all the major south Indian cities, the Goa Airport is better connected to all the major cities of the country.

Brief about the courses offered at IITDh ?

IIT Dharwad offers B.Tech, Doctoral and MS programmes. Currently, there are eight courses offered by IIT Dharwad. They are: Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Biosciences and Bioengineering, Chemistry, Mathematics, Humanities and Social Sciences, Physics.

How many slots/companies will be there per day during placement drive ?

Maximum 3 slots will be allotted per day (Internship + Placement).

From which courses students are available for recruitment ?

IIT Dharwad offers Btech courses in the fields of Computer Science and Engineering, Electrical Engineering and Mechanical Engineering. Students are available for recruitment from these three fields.

What infrastructure is available for the company for interviews/placement process ?

A multitude of classrooms as well as an air conditioned conference room is available for the purpose of interviews. Classrooms are well equipped with projectors and video conferencing capability. Two computer labs are available for conduction of online evaluation.

When will our slot for recruitment be finalised ?

Slots will be finalized within 1 week from the company's registration.
