  • PARSEC

    PARSEC 2020,the first edition of the Technology Festival of IIT Dharwad, aims to bring together the unparalleled experince of magneficient technologists and the innate curiosity of passionate students by providing a commomn platform to showcase their expertise and hone their skills. While there is no doubt that technology has taken magnanimous leaps in the past few decades, this est aimbitously aspires to see it progress in parsecs in the near future.

    VISIT PARSEC

  • DEV HACK 2019

    Believing that every engineer has the power to transform their ideas into solutions that can positively impact the world, IIT Dharwad effectuated its first 30-hour non-stop hackathon, DevHack 2019 on 11 Jan, sponsored by GitHub.

    MORE DETAILS

  • Summer of Innovation ’19

    SoI ’19 was a programme to promote open source similar in lines to GSoC and Hacktoberfest. This consisted of many open source projects in both software and hardware domain in which mentees had to make contributions. Each project had one or more mentors who guided the mentees throughout the summer remotely. More than 30 students finally qualified for swags and certificates.

    MORE DETAILS

  • PyDay

    Participate in our workshop to - Learn Python : From Beginner to Pro - over a span of two evenings.


    In our beginner session, we will build a simple Spam Classifier (without ML) together. All basics will be covered during this session. In our second session, we will introduce you to some advanced concepts and show you the true power of Python.

    MORE DETAILS

  • Cloud Study Jam

    An exciting introductory session to get started with using Google Cloud, by Girish Patil - Organizer, Google Developers Group (GDG), Huballi and Supriya Shashivasan, Co-organizer, GDG, Huballi. The event was graced by the presence of Nikhil Raichur, Community Manager, (DSC Program in India), Developer Relations Team. Google Cloud Study Jams are community-run study groups for developers centered towards the Google cloud platform.

    MORE DETAILS

  • WORKSHOP ON SOLID WORKS

    Join us to learn the best practices and gain a good grip over Solid Works, an essential for computer-aided design, solid modelling and manufacturing.


    Over the 2-hour session, we walk you through the basics of using the software and get a grip on how to explore the full depth of the software by yourself.

    MORE DETAILS

  • WORKSHOP ON ANDRIOD

    Participate in our workshop to build your First Android App over a span of two evenings.


    The Kickstart session is only for those who tried to install Android studio but failed. We strongly recommend you to try your best to install the IDE. Rome wasn't built in a day :) In our first session, we shall build an authentication app with some cool features.


    Later, in our second session we shall level up our app by integrating it with Google's FireBase. We have some interesting summer projects lined up for you.
