HR Conclave 2023


About Event

The groundbreaking third edition of HR Conclave 2023 made history at IIT Dharwad on 12th February 2023, marking the first-ever offline event of its kind. Hosted by the esteemed Career Development Council (CDC) of IIT Dharwad, the Conclave captivated audiences through a series of two riveting panel discussions featuring industry stalwarts.

  • • Moonlighting : the practice of undertaking secondary employment alongside a traditional 9-to-5 job.
  • • Downsizing : which involves reducing the size of a company by eliminating employees

This enlightening discussion on various facets of the corporate world, coupled with the panelist's invaluable guidance on transcending the role of mere manpower and becoming skillful individuals, left an indelible impression on the eager students present. The Conclave culminated in a grand felicitation of the esteemed panelists, who had shared their wisdom and experiences, and a resounding vote of thanks, symbolizing the appreciation and gratitude of all attendees.
