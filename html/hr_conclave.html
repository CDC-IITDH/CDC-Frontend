HR Conclave 2020


About Event

Apprehensive about your first step into the corporate world? Wish to check how prepared you are before making the move? Do you want to know how corporates function and how they are modeled? If you said yes, then you would not want to miss this event. With more and more companies remodeling, updating, and modernizing their organizational structure and diverging progressively from their conventional models, the Career Development Cell of IITDH has attempted to bridge the gap with its “HR Conclave” event scheduled on the 31st of October wherein illustrious and distinguished speakers of diverse backgrounds would spread consciousness and give prescience on the two major topics:

  • • Re-engineering organizations for the future: A technological insight.
  • • Upskilling the future: Bridging the gap between academics and the corporate world.
