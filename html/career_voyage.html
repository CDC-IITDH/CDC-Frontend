Career Voyage

Alumni Stories Unlocked

At IIT Dharwad, we believe in providing our students with the best tools to navigate the professional realm successfully. To further this commitment, we have conducted exclusive interviews with some of our outstanding graduates who have excelled in their internships and placements and are now flourishing in the corporate world.

The "Career Voyage" series offers a unique opportunity for our current students to gain invaluable insights, advice, and inspiration from those who have walked the path you are embarking upon. We know how essential it is to make informed decisions about career choices, and there's no better way to do so than to learn from the experiences of your own peers.

Our amazing alumni have generously shared their successes, challenges, and lessons from internships and corporate life. We'll share their stories through engaging social media posts. Get ready for exciting anecdotes, expert tips, and a behind-the-scenes look into the professional world.

Join us in celebrating the success of our alumni and leveraging their knowledge to make informed decisions for your own future!

Prateek Jain

Prateek, currently pursuing his MS in Computer Science at Columbia University in the City of New York, USA, has skillfully navigated both the academic and corporate landscapes. Publishing 2 research papers at distinguished conferences and noteworthy industry experience at esteemed organisations such as DRDO, Adobe Research, and CRED, Prateek has certainly made the IIT Dharwad community proud. In this blog post, we delve into Prateek's journey from his days at IIT Dharwad to his current pursuits in academia and industry.
