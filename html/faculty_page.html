Faculty at IIT Dharwad

"A mind is a fire to be kindled, not a vessel to be filled." - Plutarch

IIT Dharwad, as a young new institute has highly experienced and renowned professors with great expertise in their respective fields. Our faculty members hold degrees from various prestigious universities. Also, we have some visiting professors from IIT Bombay (Our Mentoring Institute).

Our professors frequently travel to give talks and attend various conferences in India and abroad. Faculty members appreciate feedback from the students and faculty advisors. This assists them in updating their teaching skills by which students are always benefited. Most of the faculty members also serve as faculty advisors and advisors to various committees in the institute. The faculty members act as mentors to the students through their involvement in extra-curricular activities to bring out best in every student.

Currently the institute has 44 Associate Professors, 5 Professors & 8 Visiting Professors from various universities. These numbers keep on increasing as institute hires more and more energetic and experienced members in its faculty.
